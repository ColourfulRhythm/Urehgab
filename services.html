Our Services

Executive Search and Headhunting

Process:

  • Consultation: We consult with clients to understand their strategic goals and culture.
  • Market Mapping: Identify target talent across industries and competitors.
  • Sourcing: Discreet headhunting using our network, research tools, and referrals.
  • Screening & Assessment: Conduct in-depth interviews, psychometrics assessment.
  • Shortlisting & Presentation: Submit top 3–5 candidates with detailed candidate reports.
  • Client Interview Coordination & Offer Management.
  • Post-placement Check-ins: For up to 3 months.

Embedded Partnership

Process:

  • Integration: Our recruiter works as an extension of the client's HR team.
  • Weekly Reporting & Strategy Calls.
  • Talent Pipeline Development: End-to-end support from job posting to onboarding.
  • Ongoing Hiring Support: Includes employer branding, market insights, and interview training.
  • Flexible Pricing Models: Retainer or monthly subscription.

Employer Branding

Process:

  • Employer Value Proposition (EVP) Discovery: Workshop with leadership and employees.
  • Content Strategy & Creation: Career pages, social media hiring campaigns, employee spotlight videos.
  • Brand Audit: Analyze and enhance the company's image on platforms like LinkedIn, Glassdoor, etc.
  • Execution & Reporting: Track engagement and impact on application quality.

Permanent and Contract Placement

Process:

  • Role Briefing Call.
  • Sourcing & Pre-screening: Using our ATS (Dover) and sourcing channels.
  • Candidate Testing: Technical and competency-based evaluations.
  • Shortlist Submission: Within 3–5 business days.
  • Offer Negotiation & Acceptance Management.
  • Follow-up Support: After placement.

HR & Capital Development

Process:

  • Needs Assessment: Identify skills gaps and HR process inefficiencies.
  • Customized Training Programs: For HR staff, managers, and employees.
  • Ongoing Support: HR advisory, policy development, and coaching.
  • Impact Tracking: Measurable outcomes linked to employee performance and retention.

ManPower Outsourcing

Process:

  • Discovery Call: We learn about your workforce needs, goals, and company culture.
  • Customized Proposal: You receive a clear service plan with costs, timelines, and expectations.
  • Talent Deployment: We recruit, vet, and onboard top-tier talent who work under your direction but remain on our payroll.
  • Ongoing Management: We handle HR support, payroll, compliance, and performance monitoring.
  • Review & Optimize: We provide regular feedback loops to ensure quality and adapt to your evolving needs.
