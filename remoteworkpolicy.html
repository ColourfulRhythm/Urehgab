Remote Work Policy

At UrehGab, we believe that great work is not confined by location. In line with our commitment to Excellence, Growth, and a Results-driven culture, we embrace a fully remote work model that empowers our team to deliver their best from anywhere.

Remote First

All roles at UrehGab are fully remote by default. Our team members have the flexibility to work from any location that enables them to stay productive, connected, and aligned with company goals.

Flexibility Hours, Clear Targets

While we promote flexibility, we also emphasize accountability. Each team member is expected to set and meet clear, measurable targets and deliver results that align with our mission.

Collaboration and Communication

We use digital tools (e.g. Zoho, Slack, Zoom, Trello, Google Workspace) to ensure seamless communication and collaboration. Regular check-ins, team meetings, and virtual huddles help maintain transparency and mutual trust.

Performance and Growth

We encourage continuous learning and provide remote access to training, mentorship, and development programs. Feedback loops are built into our processes to support both individual and team improvement.

Data Security and Professional Conduct

All employees are expected to maintain data security, confidentiality, and professional standards regardless of work location. Secure internet access, proper device usage, and adherence to company protocols are mandatory.

Wellbeing and Work-Life Balance

We recognize the importance of mental wellness and work-life balance. Flexible scheduling, regular wellness check-ins, and optional virtual socials are part of our culture.
