Hire Top Talent in 6 Simple Steps

At Urehgab, we ensure a seamless and strategic recruitment experience through a structured, transparent, and results-driven process.

1

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with a meeting to understand your hiring needs, company culture, and goals, leading to a tailored recruitment brief or service agreement.

2

Proposal & Onboarding

We align on scope and expectations through a customized proposal, formalize our partnership, and set up all tools and documents needed to launch your hiring process smoothly.

3

Role Scoping & Talent Strategy

We define the ideal candidate and craft a targeted sourcing strategy, ensuring clear timelines and alignment before talent search begins.

4

Talent Sourcing & Screening

We attract, assess, and shortlist top candidates using advanced tools and screening methods, delivering only the best-fit talent for your review.

5

Interview Setup & Offer Management

We manage seamless interview scheduling, feedback exchange, and candidate preparation then support you through selection, offer negotiation, and securing top talent ready to start.

6

Ongoing Relationship & Support

We ensure a seamless transition with structured post-placement check-ins, while building long-term partnerships through continuous hiring support, market insights, and value-driven services.
